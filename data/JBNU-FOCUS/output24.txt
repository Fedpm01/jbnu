Gang Sin-hwan, Kim Ji-hun, and Ga Sun-pyo, students of Mechanical Engineering, won the bronze prize at "2005 Original Synthesis Design Competition" under the sponsorship of the Ministry of Commerce, Industry and Energy for their sports outfit utilizing air pressure. It was held in Seoul COEX for three days starting from on November 4th. About 290 products from forty-seven universities were exhibited in the competition.					0
