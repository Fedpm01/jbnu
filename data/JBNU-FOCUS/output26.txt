제목Pansori Becomes Teaching Aid in New York                    Admin  | 2005-11-17 | 조회 3325본문 내용						Pansori Becomes Teaching Aid in New York

The complete editorialsets of Pansori that the Jeolla Cultural Institute published with the goal of popularizing and globalizing Pansori this year were selected as a teaching materials for classes at the University of Buffalo, the state University of New York. The English version of fifteen volumes of the editorial complete sets of Pansori are uned in a lecture of Park Jae-ik, an exchange professor at the State University of New York.
