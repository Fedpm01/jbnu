Chonbuk National University Museum holds a special exhibition under the title of "Cultural Scenery in Old Pictures, Jeonbuk" from April 20th to June 30th. It shows about 80 pieces of valuable data including cultural treasures in Jeonju and the pictures undiclosed to public. For more details, call at 270-3488.
