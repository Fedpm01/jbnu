CBNU had a conventional ceremony with the attendants of the university and the Honam Statistics Office March 15th, and they promised each other to work in close cooperation for their institutes. Therefore, both institutes reached a consensus for cooperating in advancement of national statistics and offering statistically-ordered information. CBNU president Suh Geo-suk said, “The national statistics are not only a cornerstone for domestic development, but also a starting line for achieving a sound consensus in our society.” He also said, “It will be possible to do scientific advanced research and analysis with the combination of the human resources of CBNU and the infrastructure of the Statistics Office.”
