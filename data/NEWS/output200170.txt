CBNU president Suh Geo-suk met Kazuyuki Nagai, the president of Chuo University, in his two days visiting to Japan and completed an official agreement for international interchange between the two schools. Through this agreement, the interchange of students and professors will be done along with a co-research project. Also, co-opening seminars and cultural interchanges through cultural activity programs will strengthen the relationship of the two universities. The IDDP (International Dual Degree Program) will lead the actual interchange of these two schools. President Suh said, "we are very happy to get into a close relationship with Chuo University, one of the best universities in Japan. We are hoping to encourage and stimulate the growth of not only both schools but also both nations.”
