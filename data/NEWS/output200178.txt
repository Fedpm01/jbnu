Son presented his study, “The progression of eco-friendly business and the prevention of the freshwater lake quality” at the 2010 annual assembly of KFWSES, which was held at the Seoul Palace Hotel. The KFWSES is a society which aims to support water-related scholars and improve the country. It also promotes various activities in water-related scholar communities as its main agenda.
