The program is a debate contest intended to settle the open-discussion culture on campus based on students’ fresh new ideas. With a total of 378 university teams participating in the preliminaries, 16 teams remained in the contest and competed in the final. Kaikero, consisting of ten students from various majors that were divided into two representative panel members and eight supportive panel members, reached the semifinals by defeating Cheonnam National University and Busan National University in a row.
