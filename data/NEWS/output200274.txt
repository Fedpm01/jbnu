 professor Kim Ki-hyun (Department of Ethics Education) was selected as a winner of the ‘Confucianism Writing Award’ by Sung Gyun Gwan. This award is given to a scholar who studies more deeply about Confucianism to realize the future-focused value of Confucianism. Professor Kim was spotlighted by Sung Gyun Gwan due to his book ‘Classical Scholar: Reason and Prospect of Life’.a aThis book describes the life and death of the classical scholar and ideology and action. In particular, Professor Kim received rave reviews as he explained the classical scholar mind connecting with the realistic life in this book.
