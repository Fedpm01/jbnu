NU student Kim Yong-kwon (the Dept. of Electrical Engineering) won the award for best student in the Small Business Experience Presentation held by the Small Business Administration. The competition was established to improve university students’ awareness of small business. Kim, who took the prize, had training in a small business during summer vacation this year. He had a good evaluation to pass the general feeling in the course and for future career and employment plans.a aKim said, “I had duped the prejudice of small businesses due to this occasion and it is very helpful to plan my future.”
