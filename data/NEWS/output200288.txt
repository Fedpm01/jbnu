Gyeong-sik (sophomore, The Dept. of International Trade) will receive a presidential citation as a winner of “outstanding individual of Korea”. Kim’s superb school record and volunteer work to introduce Korean culture abroad were highly praised as he was chosen a winner. He taught how to use cell phones to the elderly and instructed Korean and Taekwondo to natives in Mongolia and the Philippines as an educational volunteer. a aHe also devoted himself to letting many foreigners know Korean culture while participating as a member of ‘Knightage of Hallyu’, which was hosted by Gyeonggi-do. A winner of “outstanding individual of Korea” is allowed to participate in overseas training programs as well as get a presidential citation and a three million won scholarship.a a
