NU awarded an honorary doctorate in literature to a poet Ko-En, who was a great master of Korean literature. CBNU had an awarding ceremony on March 30th. Poet Ko-En was nominated for the Nobel Prize for Literature many times and is a great name in literature over the world. In the ceremony, President Suh said, “Today this ceremony recognizes the great teacher who worried about the future of the nation. Again, thank you for your devotion, and we show respect to your whole life. After the ceremony, the poet had a presentation about ‘The world of Poet, Ko En and today’s Korean poetry.’ a
