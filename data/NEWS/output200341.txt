Public awareness of CBNU is rapidly increasing. This is based on the result that 40% of freshmen came from other cities. From Seoul and the capital area, the number of students has increased threefold. Also, the students coming from ChungCheon-Do have tripled, and students from the Yeongnam and Honam areas have doubled as well. Recently, CBNU jumped into Korea’s Top 10 Universities for two years straight. Also, CBNU research funds topped 100 billion won from the diversity of national projects.a a
