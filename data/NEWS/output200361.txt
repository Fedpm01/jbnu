CBNU Science Education Institute for the Gifted has built a network with the Science Education Institutes for the Gifted of the other universities in the Honam area. aThe five institutes including our university’s made an agreement to build networks. According to the agreement, all institutions share their own classrooms, laboratories, professors and education programs. aDirector Ahn Byeong-jun said, “This agreement and workshop have meaning in building a cooperative system and exchanging information among the universities while refraining from unnecessary competition.” a
