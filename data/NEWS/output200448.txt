		CBNU, marking its 64th anniversary, awarded scholarships to self-improving students on the basis of their merits on November 10.  There was an awarding ceremony of scholarship in Jinsudang, and the school awarded a million won per person to all 64 students.aThe students who got the scholarship were selected by careful evaluation among the applicants who were working actively to improve themselves.aCBNU President Suh Geo-suk said, “All of you have infinite potential for being global leaders. Please do your best to be the most outstanding students in CBNU.” Also, President Suh spent time with students, listening to their impressions and suggestions.
