Yang Soon-jong of STAR SAVINGS BANK has donated a scholarship of one hundred million won to CBNU. It is designed to practice the management philosophy that states, 'A company has to contribute to public welfare by training and educating an employee.’ a aSTAR SAVINGS COMPANY has donated scholarships totalling three hundred million won to high schools located in Jeonbuk every year. CBNU is going to select five students who study hard in spite of poor family background and give 'STAR SAVINGS BANK' scholarships through the intention of the contributor until they graduate. President Yang said, "STAR SAVINGS BANK is an outstanding banking facility through stable asset management and tries to do its duty to contribute to training employees. I hope that the money is used for students who devote themselves to their dreams." a
