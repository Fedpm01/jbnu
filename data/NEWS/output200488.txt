olar Cell Center for R&D Manpower Education ais progressing the student exchange program with Nagasaki University for the purpose of cultivating men of talent in the new renewable energy field. Ten students were given an education about solar battery material and nanotechnology study in Nagasaki University. Through this exchange, CBNU looks forward to sharing of new technology in the new renewable energy field and trade of cultivating men of ability. As new renewable energy has received the attention of a wide range of the public because of rising dangerousness of nuclear power like with the Tohoku Region Pacific Coast Earthquake, both colleges prepared this program.
