Professor Lee's paper ”A Study on the Meaning and Mount Effect of the Twelve Peaks of Musan in Yongho Garden, Jinju,” received a favorable review at this conference. This paper was published in the academic journal of the Korean Institute of Traditional Landscape Architecture. It was favorably reviewed because the professor systematically analyzed the Twelve Peaks of Musan's value and meaning. Meanwhile, the Korean Institute of Traditional Landscape Architecture was founded for the purpose of developing landscape architecture's culture by combining traditional landscape architecture with today's landscape architecture.
