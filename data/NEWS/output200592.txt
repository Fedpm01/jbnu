Institute of Science Education for the Gifted and Talented (ISEGT) had an entrance ceremony on March 30th. This institute was established in 1998 and operated as an affiliated organization of CBNU. It has become a representative institution of Jeollabuk-do which provides science education for the talented. aChoi Jong-oh, a doctor in the Korean Research Institute of Standards and Science, gave a lecture about the beginning and the end of science technology to the students. Also, Doctor Lim Jae-keun of Daejeon Seongmo Elementary School delivered a lecture explaining the appropriate attitude and role of the parents. Professors of CBNU will provide a year-long course of advanced education of math and science to the students. Besides this, students will do science experiments in person as well as learn theoretical education.
