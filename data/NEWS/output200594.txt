For this project, CBNU is recruiting 22 trainees online and offline until May 12th and will provide training courses to them. Jeollabuk-do actively promotes the seed industry as it has a ripple effect on other industries such as agriculture, bio industry, and food industry. Furthermore, to cultivate professionals of this field becomes a more important thing as a seed valley has been confirmed to be established in Gimje. Professor Yun Seong-joong in the College of Agriculture and Life Science will provide on-site practical education and theories about the seed industry from June 3rd to August 23rd. In addition, the total educational expenses are provided by the government.
