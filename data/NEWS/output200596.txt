 recently got selected by the government to manage the establishment of personalized business, which is for finding pre-entrepreneurs with promising ideas and for supporting them. As a result, a workshop was held on April 4th in CBNU. There were presentations to clearly inform the meaning of the business and to advise on the way of writing a business plan necessary for funds. The administrator of the Business Incubator Center of CBNU, an operating organization of the establishment of personalized business, said that he prepared the workshop to assist and lead preliminary founders who have faith in their businesses but go through difficulties to get starting capital. The administrator said, “We hope that the participants in this workshop have worthwhile opportunities to decide the direction of the establishment for each of them, and to get information about taking the funds from the government.”
