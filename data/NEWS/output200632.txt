 Solehati, a doctoral student in the Department of Industrial and Information Systems Engineering, received the ‘Best Presentation Award’ in the 2013 Flexible Automation & Intelligent Manufacturing conference (FAIM 2013). This conference has authority in the fields of mechanical engineering and industrial engineering. The Best Presentation Award is a prize for the best thesis presenter participating in this conference. Solehati presented his paper, ‘Geometrical Optimization of Micro-Mixer with Wavy Structure Design for Chemical Processes using Taguchi Method’, with his advising professor, Bae Jun-soo.
