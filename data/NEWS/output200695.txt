 Dong-pyo, from the Department of Mechanical System Engineering, has been named as a head of engineering for the National Research Foundation of Korea (NRF). With this assignment, he will manage the engineering department in general from planning to outcome. He took a role as a professional member at NRF, and he is also working as a Korean Journal member at SCOPUS, which is the world’s largest paper citation index. He said, “I will be responsible for contributing to basic research for the national development of engineering.”
