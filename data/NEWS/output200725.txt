CBNU carried out a fire drill with Deokjin Fire Station at its residence hall on May 8th. In this fire drill, students trained for a fire situation using virtual reality. In particular, the fire drill was run in the three languages of Korean, English, and Chinese for foreign students. Also, CBNU carried out a fire drill at its library and checked the safety condition of 167 contiguous facilities. In addition, a safety education class was held for all employees to cope with accidents quickly. Secretary-general Han Sang-shin said, "I have checked all facilities of CBNU,” and added, "I will make a secure reaction system for disaster through students’ actual training."
