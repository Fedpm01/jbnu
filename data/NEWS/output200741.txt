 student Kim Eun-seok, who is in the Department of Korean Music, was awarded the grand prize at the 14th Iksan National Pansori Contest. From 30th to 31st Semtember, this Pansori Contest was held at Iksan Somri Culture & Arts Center to cherish the memory of national singer Jung Jung-ryul. In the preliminary round on the first day, Kim sang Sugungga so passionately that it received high praise from judges. On the 31st, he sang one part of Jeokbyeokga. He got 99 points and won the grand prize. Kim said, “This is my first time winning the grand prize. I am happy to produce good results despite nervousness.” He also added, “I will try more and more, so I will grow up as a master singer who touches the heart of the audience.”
