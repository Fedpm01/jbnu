eptember 17th, CBNU made a construction and transportation agreement with Chonnam National University (CNU), Iksan Regional Construction Management Administration (IRCMA), and the Honam Construction and Transportation Technology Community Center (HCTTCC) for developing construction and transportation technology in the Honam area. These four organizations would share information about construction and transportation and decide to collaborate for advances in construction and transportation technology. The organizations decided to cooperate through policy support, interchange of technology, and professional manpower training and by sharing facilities and equipment. They also agreed to bear a part in growing HCTTCC to ad R&D hub of construction and transportation technology in the Honam area.
