Department of Clothing and Textiles held 19th fashion festival to give Jeonju citizens an opportunity to enjoy culture and art. The fashion festival was opened on September 30th at CBNU Cultural Center and its theme was 'TIMELESS'. Eternal value which is timeless was expressed through creative works of art made by 24 students such as clothes containing image of Africa and those inspired by gladiator from Rome. Chairman of the preparatory committee, Kim Eun-ji said, "This fashion festival is a showcase of our creative works. I want to thank professors and colleagues who participated in all process and I will try more to achieve a higher goal."
