ecember 2014, CBNU LINC (Leaders in Industry University Cooperation) and ICEE (Innovation Center for Engineering Education) both held the “2014 Capstone Design · Start-up Exposition.” At the exposition, all 318 students’ 67 pieces of various ideas were shown. The grand prize went to a team from the Division of Mechanical Design Engineering. This team made a bicycle parking brake that can park stably on mountains or any slanted places without finding a bicycle holder. At the awards ceremony, President Lee Nam-ho said, “Capstone design is the education program that CBNU put a lot of effort into.” He also said, “By seeing the creative ideas of students, I think our country’s future is very promising.”
