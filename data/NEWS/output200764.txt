Many various products including a chocolate using local foods in Jeollabukdo, accessories made of pieces of cloth, and hanok-shaped stand for Dutch coffee were displayed at the festival. Besides these, there was the sale of products like accessories and recycled objects made by students. Lee Nam-ho, the president of CBNU, said, “The student foundation festival is a place where ideas and passions of students congregate. I will support the student foundation and help students grow to be outstanding people.”
