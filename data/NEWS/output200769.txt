This business is carried out to promote a base for start-up by supporting various things such as startup education, planning items, and commercialization. For this reason, CBNU will support outstanding founders and various start-up programs, investing up to 2.8 billion won over three years. CBNU will discover excellent the ideas and technology of business starters and will provide mentoring, trial manufactured goods, and marketing using infrastructure that CBNU has accumulated. President Lee Nam-ho said, “CBNU will try to raise excellent founders and enterprise aimed at construction of start-up cluster.”
