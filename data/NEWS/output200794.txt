arch 19th, CBNU and Jeonju city made an agreement concerning close cooperation with each other. On this day, CBNU President Lee Nam-ho and the Mayor of Jeonju, Kim Seung-su, signed to co-develop local business and national projects. With this agreement, they promised to help each other in areas like ecology, culture, and so on. In particular, they will push forward on the carbon and agricultural life industries that are fostered strategically. President Lee Nam-ho said, “CBNU will build the most Korean-style campus and try our best to create the best campus to the local residents. I hope that we will be able to seek practical measures in a diverse way.”
