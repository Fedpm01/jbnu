CBNU Career Support Center has developed an application, “Mobile JOB,” to help students’ preparation for employment systematically. Mainly, it consists of notices and employment information. In the notice section, students can check the academic calendar, notices of employment, and schedules for special lecture or seminar. The section for employment information provides information according to the topic of business scale, domestic and foreign areas, and industrial classification. Also, they can check their Grand Person Points, scholarships, and award-winning career through this app. To use this application, download the app after searching for “CBNU employment information” in the App store.
