 LINC (Leaders in Industry University Cooperation) has achieved a good business outcome. LINC was assigned about 5.4 billion won, which was the biggest amount in Korea. LINC is doing their best for establishing a leading model of industry cooperation and training talented individuals in the bio-energy field. LINC has been evaluated as the best business group in Korea, receiving the highest scores in a row on recent assessments. Professor Seol Kyoung-won, the headmaster of LINC, said, "CBNU LINC is trying to raise the employment rate through Industry Cooperation. We will do our best to contribute to local development by training talented people who are specialized in local strategic business fields."
