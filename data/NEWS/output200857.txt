College of Veterinary Medicine recently concluded an agreement with the Animal Clinic Research Foundation of Japan (ACRF). ACRF is a research establishment which is the best in the field of animal clinics in Japan. Through the agreement, they will exchange academic information and students. They will also collaborate in joint research about mutual interests. Kim Nam-soo, the dean of the College of Veterinary Medicine, said, “We are at the highest level of infrastructure and competitive education. Moreover, ACRF has the most powerful authority on animal clinics in Japan. I hope that we will help develop each other.”
