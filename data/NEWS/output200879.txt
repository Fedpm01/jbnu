In attracting this research center, CBNU became the only university in the country to have a research center and to proceed with a global study about metamodeling. After the opening ceremony, CBNU and OMiLAB KOREA Research Center made an agreement for the exchange in the field of study with Dimitris Karagiannis, a professor in the University of Vienna, the executive vice president of External Cooperation, Yang Moon-sik, and the director of OMiLAB KOREA, Lee Moon-kun. The CBNU College of Engineering and University of Vienna of Computer Science will cooperate to have collaborative research, exchange of teachers and researchers, seminars, joint conference attendance and education.
