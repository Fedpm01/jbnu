 LINC (Leaders in Industry University Cooperation) is situated in the field of inventive education. CBNU LINC introduced education for international TRIZ (Teoriya Resheniya Izobretatelskikh Zadatch) certification. TRIZ is the theory of solving inventive problems by finding fundamental contradictions. CBNU LINC trains students to strengthen creativity competence. Many CBNU students who had this training received various prizes. Professor Seol Kyoung-won, the headmaster of LINC, said, "I'm very happy because a lot of CBNU students achieved good results in many contests which evaluate creative problem solving abilities. We will progress inventive education such as the international TRIZ certification training and action learning education constantly to cultivate creative talents who can solve corporate problems."
