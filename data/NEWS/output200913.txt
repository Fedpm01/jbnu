essor Kim Hyun-jung in the Department of Mineral Resources & Energy Engineering recently won the Young Scientist Award in the 2016 Society for Mining, Metallurgy & Exploration, which was held in Phoenix, Arizona, in the United States. Professor Kim is the first Korean to win this award since it was first given at Industrial Minerals & Aggregates Division in 1985. Professor Kim said, “I was able to win the Young Scientist Award because of all my colleague researchers and graduate students who conducted research hard in a harsh environment. I’m very glad to inform that the field of resource disposal and mineral application research is being carried out actively in Korea to foreign researchers through this opportunity.”
