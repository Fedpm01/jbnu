The College of Veterinary Medicine has an infectious diseases research laboratory which leads the development of new pharmaceuticals about Avian Influenza (AI), brucellosis, and other incurable diseases. Furthermore, the CBNU School of Dentistry has conducted various research projects like Brain Korea 21 (BK21), and, in particular, every applicant has passed the Dentist Examination since 2014. CBNU President Lee Nam-ho said, “This ranking means that CBNU can be a world-best university, and I will establish long term strategies for the maturity of CBNU.”
