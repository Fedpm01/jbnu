NU held a professional education camp about New & Renewable Energy during vacation. The camp is for growing professional talents that will lead many companies. Leaders in Industry-university Cooperation (LINC) provided professional experience in industrial settings and opportunities for students in New & Renewable Energy to acquire technology. In addition, companies could get professional manpower. Throughout the camp, CBNU students learned expertise that the industry actually demands and witnessed the flow of industrial technology. They will next visit industrial settings related to new and renewable energy in Jeju. The leader of LINC business said, "New energy is very important because it will decide the future of the region and country.
