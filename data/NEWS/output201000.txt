The Korea-China University Forum is a forum that is held every year at universities in Korea and China taking turns. Eleven of Korea’s universities, including CBNU, and 16 of China’s universities participated in this forum. There was a time to debate and present about innovation and university development, university culture and cultivation of talent, and so on. At the forum, the Executive Vice President of External Cooperation, Yang Moon-sik, participated as a debater and talked about transmission of traditional culture and innovation and university development. He said, “At a time of risky existence of universities since the school age population is decreasing, Korean universities have to innovate. Also, universities have to fulfill their social responsibilities, leaving great cultural tradition to posterity”
