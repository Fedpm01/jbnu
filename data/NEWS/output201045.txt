For regional development, Kwak thinks developing a regionally based university’s competitiveness and students’ talents should be done in advance. Therefore, Kwak contributed to developing CBNU as the most prestigious university in Korea. President Lee Nam-ho delivered an appreciation plaque during the development fund ceremony.
