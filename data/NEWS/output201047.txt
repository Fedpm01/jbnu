Starting from 2012, CBNU has been qualified for five years in a row and the certificate is valid till 2020. The benefits of the certificate are that preferential treatment is given at university support projects and the quality of the university can be guaranteed overseas. The President of the CBNU Office of International Cooperation, Yoon Myeong-sook, said, “CBNU is qualified as a good university for foreign students by the certification and will work hard to make CBNU a better environment for foreign students.”
