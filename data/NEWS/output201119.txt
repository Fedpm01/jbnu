This fair is prepared for the sake of coexistent cooperation in the fourth industrial revolution in Jeollabuk-do and progressing variety activities such as automotive industry counseling of export support, a future car theme park, a gallery of techniques, capstone design, and competitive idea exhibition. The President of Leaders in Industry-university Cooperation, Ko Young-ho, said, “For students’ fresh thinking and improvement of their problem-solving skill, we’ll continuously run education on creative thinking and problem-solving ability,” adding, “We hope for the economy of Jeollabuk-do to co-develop.”
