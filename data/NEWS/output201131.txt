This competition was held for two days at the Hongwon Training Institute in Paju, Geyonggi-do and there were 706 students from 32 universities, who presented cases of a year’s research and shared their innovative ideas on preventing gambling problems. Dice conducts campaigns regularly to prevent gambling problems in the community and promotes the teenage gambling problem helpline “1336” by visiting middle and high schools. They have been receiving excellence prizes every year at this competition and they are recognized as a model university club for the gambling problem prevention activities.
