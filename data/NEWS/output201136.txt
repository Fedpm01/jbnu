 Industrial Cooperation Foundation and KBSCOA (Korea Big and Strong small business CO-win Association) made a business agreement to build an industrial-educational cooperation. These institutions promised to cooperate in many types of businesses such as technology and administrative consultation, education for startup and technological development. In particular, KBSCOA supports small but strong companies in Korea to become more competitive by cooperating with large companies. Moreover, KBSCOA aims to make more than one million creative business items for small but strong companies in Korea. Lee Cheul-ro, the head of CBNU Industrial Cooperation Foundation said, “Through this contract, I will support many family companies of CBNU to become excellent small hidden companies. Furthermore, I wish this close connection continues on for both institutions to develop together.”
