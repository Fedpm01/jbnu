It was an opportunity to learn about the artist's philosophy on humans and nature, which is centered on the modern and diverse transformation of traditional paintings. In 1989, he was widely known as an “Umbrella Artist”. He proved his creative spirit by carving out a new method beyond widely known and successful ways. After 15 years, in 2004, he again held an exhibition on the subject “Umbrella”, and over the years, the meaning of the umbrella has become deeper and wider. His work “Umbrella” shows his respect for human life and the environment. This exhibition showed the classic Korean and Chinese masterpieces of Korea and China’s by remaking the umbrella.
