Los Alamos National Laboratory-Chonbuk National University Engineering Institute Korea (LANL - CBNU) and Hyundai Motor Company recently signed a collaboration agreement for developing new technology. In this cooperative project, the laboratory is working on a diversity of world class the most advanced nondestructive inspection technique, and Hyundai Motor Company are going to develop the application technology of motor that cannot be solved by traditional approach. Both institutions will conduct research and design at a global facility, with the necessary equipment and people infrastructure. Los Alamos National Laboratory-Chonbuk National University Engineering Institute Korea contributes to the local industry by conducting industry-university research. Also, they build various complex systems connecting wind power, flight, drone, carbon fiber and electronic components.
